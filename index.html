NutriTrack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

NutriTrack is an offline, on-device calorie and protein tracker. We do not create accounts, do not collect personal identifiers, and do not send your data to our servers.

Information We Collect

  • Nutrition data you enter (foods, meals, goals, daily selections)
  • Export files you optionally generate (CSV)
  • No names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers are collected.

How Your Data Is Used

  • Stored locally on your device to show your logs, totals, and history.
  • Export is user-initiated; files are created locally and shared only when you choose.

Storage & Sharing

  • Data is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not transmit, sell, or share your data with third parties.
  • If you choose to export/share a file, you control where it goes (e.g., email, drive).

Permissions

  • Storage/File access: used only to save/export your data when you request it.
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or other sensitive permissions are used.

Analytics & Tracking

  • No analytics SDKs, ads, or third-party trackers are integrated.
  • App store platforms may collect basic diagnostics per their own policies.

Security

Data stays on-device. Protect your device and backups to keep your data safe.

Children

NutriTrack can be used by all ages. The app does not collect personal identifiers or require an account. If a parent/guardian wishes to remove data entered by a child, entries can be deleted in-app or by uninstalling the app (data is stored only on the device).

Your Choices

  • You can delete entries, meals, and goals within the app.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; changes will be posted here with an updated “Last updated” date.
